# Contributing to veil

Thanks for contributing. A few terms so contributions fit the project's
licensing model.

## Licensing of contributions

veil is open-core: the free edition is **source-available under PolyForm
Noncommercial 1.0.0**, and there is a separate **commercial ("Pro") edition**.

By submitting a contribution (a pull request, patch, or any change), you:

1. Certify you wrote it or otherwise have the right to submit it — the
   Developer Certificate of Origin (https://developercertificate.org/); and
2. Grant Marc Stampfli a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free, irrevocable
   license to use, reproduce, modify, sublicense, and distribute your
   contribution as part of veil **under any license**, including both the
   PolyForm Noncommercial free edition and the commercial Pro edition.

You keep the copyright in your contribution — this is a license grant, not an
assignment. It exists so your contribution can ship in both editions.

(For a more formal process later, this can be replaced by a signed CLA.)

## Using veil

Per the free-edition license (`LICENSE`): you may use, modify, and share veil
for **noncommercial** purposes. You may **not** sell it or use it commercially
without a separate license — contact marc@mstampfli.com for commercial terms.
